Who I Am

About Me

I'm a third-year Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) student at Liaquat University of Medical & Health Sciences (LUMHS), Jamshoro, with a deep commitment to Oral Surgery, Oral Medicine, and evidence-based clinical practice.

My clinical training spans 40+ supervised tooth extractions, proficiency in ten distinct nerve block techniques (IANB, PSA, MSA, ASA, long buccal, lingual, greater palatine, nasopalatine, mental, and infraorbital), composite restorations, and radiographic interpretation — built across internships at Bhitai Dental & Medical College and Advance Dental Care Centre.

Outside the clinic, I co-investigate two ongoing cross-sectional research studies in paediatric oral health and dental anxiety, and I lead peer-learning initiatives that have supported 200+ BDS students through the Study Aid Project at LUMHS.

"My goal is to combine strong clinical technique with meaningful research to improve dental care access and outcomes in underserved communities across Pakistan."
